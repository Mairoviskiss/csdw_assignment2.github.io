- Ingredients -

  • Starter: 100ml water and 25g flour
  • 125ml warm milk
  • 25g fresh yeast or 7g packet
  • 30g sugar
  • 60g melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 450g flour
  • Also: 1 egg yolk mixed with 2 tbsp of milk, Nutella.

- Method -

  1. Make the starter: heat flour and water over low heat until it looks like a mirror glaze. Leave to cool.
  2. Mix warm milk, yeast and sugar.
  3. Add starter, sieved flour, salt and eggs to the above mix until the ingredients almost come together.
  4. Add melted cooled butter and knead for around 10 minutes.
  5. Leave covered with a cloth in a warm area for an hour. The dough should at least double in size.
  6. Heat the oven to 190 degrees.
  7. Divide the dough into 4 part, roll out each part, fold it over, roll out again, put a spoonful of Nutella and roll it up. The rolls should be of the width of the baking tray.
  8. Put all the rolls into the tray as shown in photos and leave to rise for 30 minutes.
  9. After 30 minutes put some egg yolk with milk on top of the rolls and bake for 20-25 minutes in the oven or until golden.
