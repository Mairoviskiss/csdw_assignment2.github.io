Ugali, nyama choma and kachumbari

By Allan Matwetwe

- Ingredients -

Ugali

  • 1 litre water
  • 320g maize flour

Kachumbari

  • 350ml warm water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 onions
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 200g corriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Nyama Choma

  • 350 ml warm water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 leg of goat (preferably hind leg)
  • 1 rack of goat ribs
