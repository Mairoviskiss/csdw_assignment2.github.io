Spicy mediterranean chicken with rice and roast veg 🌶🌶

By Aurelia Luszcz

- Ingredients -

  • Breast on a bone
  • Veg of your choice
  • Jasmine rice
  • Hot paprika powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Rosemary
  • Marjoram
  • Salt and pepper
  • Additionally: olive oil or sunflower spray

- Method -

  1. Rub the chicken breasts in the spices and olive oil/sunflower spray.
  2. Put the chicken in a pan, cover with tinfoil, and into the oven at 190 degrees for an hour
  3. Chop up the veg of your choice (carrots, spinach, broccoli, peas etc.).
  4. After an hour, put the veg in the same pan as the chicken, sprinkle with rosemary and salt and put back in the oven for 20 minutes without covering with foil.
  5. Prepare the rice as per instructions on the packet.
  6. When the veg is soft and the chicken's juices run clear, it's ready to be served!
